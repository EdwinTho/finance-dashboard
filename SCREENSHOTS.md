## How to Take Screenshots:

1. Open app: http://localhost:8000
2. Load sample data (settings menu)
3. Press `Cmd + Shift + 4` on Mac
4. Drag to select area
5. Save as: `screenshot-dashboard.png` etc.
6. Compress images: https://tinypng.com
7. Upload to repo or use imgur.com
8. Update README.md with image URLs
